Client side***
 - Register and login
 - Search and find the nearest salon
 - Check the prices, reviews and ratings
 - Book an appointment
   - Check how many people have already made appointments
   - Make online payments
   - Generate invoice
 - Rate the salon
   - Hygiene rating
   - Hairstyle rating
   - Experience rating
 - Give reviews
   - Add comments
   - Add picture of the hairstyle
 - Get notification about promotions from the salons

Service provider***
 - Register and login
   - Should agree that their salon will be rated and 
     (will not be able to rate themselves)
 - Add details of the salon
   - Name
   - Address
   - Pricing for items and descriptions
   - Banking details for online payments
 - Get notification when someone books an appointment
 - Have its own profile
 - Create promotions
